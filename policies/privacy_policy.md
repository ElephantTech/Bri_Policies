# Privacy Policy for Bri

**Effective date:** 19th June 2026
**App:** Bri
**Company:** Elephant Technologies
**Contact:** support@elephnt.tech

## Plain-English summary

Bri is designed to be private and local-first.

Your recovery information stays on your device. Elephant Technologies does not collect, view, store, sell, or share the notes, symptoms, trends, reports, Health data, or other recovery information you enter into Bri.

You stay in control. You choose what to record, what to delete, whether to back up your device, and whether to export or share information with clinicians or others.

The only personal information Elephant Technologies may receive is information you choose to send outside the app, such as an email, feedback message, app store review, screenshot, or support request.

## 1. About Bri

Bri is a mobile wellbeing and self-management app for people navigating concussion recovery.

Bri helps you record information, notice possible patterns, prepare for appointments, and discuss your recovery with clinicians or other trusted people.

Bri is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not a substitute for care from a qualified health professional. Bri does not tell you whether you are safe to return to sport, work, driving, school, or other activities.

If you are worried about your symptoms, safety, or a change in your condition, seek medical advice or urgent help.

## 2. What this policy covers

This policy applies only to the Bri mobile app.

It does not cover the Elephant Technologies website, newsletter, contact forms, partnership forms, consultancy services, or other support channels. Those services may have their own privacy policy.

## 3. Information stored in Bri

You may choose to enter information such as daily notes, symptoms, overall feeling, triggers, events, appointments, medications, questions, goals, and clinician appointment preparation notes.

Bri may also use optional device-based information, such as Apple Health data, if you choose to allow access.

This information is stored locally on your device. Elephant Technologies does not receive it.

## 4. No analytics or tracking

Bri does not use analytics, advertising trackers, third-party crash reporting, telemetry, or behavioural tracking.

Elephant Technologies does not receive information about how you use Bri during normal app use.

## 5. Your control over your data

Because Bri stores your recovery data locally, you manage that data directly in the app or on your device.

You can correct, update, export, or delete your app data using the app features available to you. You may also delete app data by deleting the app from your device, subject to your device and backup settings.

Bri may provide optional local database encryption and optional app lock protection, such as PIN, biometric unlock, or device-based security features.

Bri does not provide its own cloud backup service. If you choose to use Apple, Google, or other device backup services, your Bri data may be backed up according to your device settings and the backup provider’s privacy policy.

If your data has been included in a device or cloud backup, you may need to manage or delete those backups through your Apple, Google, device, or backup settings.

## 6. Optional app features

Bri may allow optional access to Apple Health or similar device-based data sources. This access is controlled through your device settings. Health data used by Bri stays on your device and is not sent to Elephant Technologies.

Bri may include an optional “speak your entry” feature. Speech-to-text is processed locally on your device and is not sent to Elephant Technologies or an external AI provider by Bri.

Bri may generate summaries, trend views, prompts, and appointment preparation screens from information you enter. These are intended to help you reflect and prepare for conversations with clinicians.

Automated outputs may be incomplete or imperfect. Please review them carefully before relying on them or sharing them.

## 7. Learning content and app updates

Bri may download publicly available learning content, tool content, or app resources from Elephant Technologies servers.

These downloads do not require an account. Elephant Technologies does not intentionally collect your recovery information, entries, symptoms, notes, Health data, reports, or clinician preparation information when this content is downloaded.

## 8. Notifications

Bri does not use push notifications.

Any reminders or notifications provided by Bri are generated locally on your device. Notification content is not sent to Elephant Technologies.

## 9. Exports and sharing

Bri may let you generate reports or summaries for clinicians, ACC, insurers, employers, family members, carers, or other people.

Exports are generated locally on your device. Elephant Technologies does not receive a copy.

You control whether to export or share information. Once you share information outside Bri, Elephant Technologies cannot control how the recipient, app, service, or organisation handles it.

## 10. Feedback and support

If you contact Elephant Technologies by email, app store review, or another support channel, Elephant Technologies may receive the information you choose to provide.

This may include your email address, name, message, screenshots, attachments, device information, app version information, or anything else you include.

Please do not send sensitive health or recovery information to Elephant Technologies unless you are comfortable including it in your message.

Elephant Technologies may use support and feedback messages to respond to you, fix issues, improve Bri, and keep reasonable records.

Support and feedback may be stored using email or productivity providers such as Google or Microsoft. These providers may store or process information outside New Zealand.

## 11. How long support information is kept

Elephant Technologies keeps support emails and feedback only for as long as reasonably needed for support, product improvement, safety, legal, dispute-resolution, and record-keeping purposes.

Where practical, Elephant Technologies will delete or anonymise support and feedback information when it is no longer needed.

You can contact Elephant Technologies at support@elephnt.tech to ask for access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information Elephant Technologies holds about you in support or feedback records.

## 12. Sharing and disclosure

Elephant Technologies does not disclose your Bri recovery data because Elephant Technologies does not receive or hold that data.

Elephant Technologies may disclose support or feedback information only where reasonably necessary, such as to respond to your request, work with service providers, comply with the law, protect safety or legal rights, or manage a business transfer or restructure.

Elephant Technologies does not sell your personal information.

## 13. Children and young people

Bri is intended for adults.

If Bri is used by a child or young person, a parent, guardian, caregiver, or responsible adult should decide whether Bri is appropriate and supervise use.

## 14. Your privacy rights

For Bri data stored only on your device, Elephant Technologies cannot access, correct, export, or delete that data for you. You must manage it directly in the app, on your device, or through your device backup settings.

For personal information Elephant Technologies does hold, such as support emails or feedback you send to Elephant Technologies, you may contact support@elephnt.tech to ask to access, correct, or delete that information.

If you are in New Zealand and are not satisfied with how your privacy request is handled, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

## 15. Security

Bri is designed to reduce privacy risk by keeping recovery data local to your device and avoiding analytics, telemetry, advertising trackers, third-party crash reporting, and cloud accounts.

No app, device, database, backup, export, or communication method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should keep your device updated, use a strong device passcode, enable app lock features where available, and take care when exporting or sharing reports.

## 16. Changes to this policy

Elephant Technologies may update this privacy policy from time to time.

If changes are significant, Elephant Technologies will take reasonable steps to make the updated policy available in the app or through another appropriate channel.
